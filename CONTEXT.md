## CAROUSEL VISUAL PREFERENCES

### Aesthetic
- Light/white background + dark text (primary)
- Gradient cool tones (blue, purple, teal) for accent slides
- High contrast black + white with one accent color for data callouts
- Muted/pastel minimal for quote cards
- Two-tone brand color split for cover slides

### Typography
- **Heading fonts (rotate):** Sora, Outfit, Clash Display, DM Sans, Bricolage Grotesque, Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body font:** DM Sans or Plus Jakarta Sans (clean, readable)
- **Heading size:** Large and bold (takes up 40-60% of slide)
- **Body size:** Comfortable reading, never cramped

### Slide Count
- Default: 5-7 slides
- Auto-adjust based on content depth

### Slide Layouts (Mix across slides within one carousel)
- Headline + 2-3 bullet points
- Bold headline only (1 big statement per slide)
- Headline + short paragraph (2-3 lines)
- Icon or number + short label (infographic style)
- Quote card (big quote, source at bottom)
- Before/After or comparison layout
- Step number + insight (Step 1: Do this)
- Data callout (big stat + explanation)

### Cover Slide Style
- Auto-detect best cover style for the topic:
  - Bold question as headline for educational content
  - Surprising stat for data-driven content
  - Contrarian statement for opinion content
- Must stop the scroll. This IS the hook on carousels.

### Last Slide
- Always include a closing CTA slide
- "Follow for more SEO insights" or topic-specific nudge
- Keep it clean — one line, not cluttered
