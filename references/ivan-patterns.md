# Post Style Inspiration

Inspiration sources for YOUR posts. Not templates. Not formats to copy. Just patterns that work.

---

## What We Admire (Adapted for SEO Content)

### From Ivan Davidov — Code Snippet Posts (Favourite)
- Real code/config screenshots on dark backgrounds with syntax highlighting
- Post text explains the WHY, the image shows the HOW
- Keep text SHORT when an image carries the value (100-150 words)
- First line creates curiosity about the pattern

**For SEO adaptation:** Instead of code snippets, use schema markup screenshots, Search Console data screenshots, crawl analysis visuals, or structured data examples on dark backgrounds.

### From Ivan — Variety of Hooks
He rotates between question hooks, personal intent, relatable pain, direct statements, and announcements. Never the same format twice.

### From Stefan Judis — Curiosity-Driven Openings
- "I wonder if..." / "Did I talk to you about the joy of..."
- Genuine curiosity, not rhetorical. Makes you feel like you're thinking alongside him.
- Positions as a learner sharing discoveries, not an expert lecturing.

**For SEO adaptation:** "I wonder how many of my pages are actually being surfaced in AI Overviews..." or "Did I tell you about the experiment I ran on schema markup last month?"

## Hook Examples (SEO/AEO/Content Strategy)

**Contrast:** "Rankings went up. Traffic went down."
**Number:** "83% of featured snippets changed owners in 6 months."
**Pain:** "Have you ever spent a week optimizing a page only to watch it drop?"
**Story:** "Last month I ran an experiment on 40 blog posts."
**Curiosity:** "The one schema change that tripled our click-through rate."
**Question:** "Is your content actually ranking for AI answers?"
**Challenge:** "Stop obsessing over keyword difficulty scores."

## WHAT NOT TO DO

- Copy Ivan's all-lowercase style (he actually uses normal caps in real posts)
- Force one-sentence-per-line on every post
- Always use "pov:" or "ps:" patterns
- Use "💾 save / ♻️ repost" generic CTAs
- Follow any rigid template structure
- Make every post sound the same
- Exceed word limits because the content "needs more space"
